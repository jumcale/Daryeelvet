About Us

Founded in July 2023, Daryeel Veterinary Pharmacy serves as a cornerstone for livestock health in Berbera and the Sahil Region. We are dedicated to providing high-quality pharmaceutical services, expert consultations, and comprehensive support to pastoralists, farmers, and livestock traders.

Our goal is to improve animal health and productivity through accessible, reliable, and professional veterinary care.

Our Mission

To provide compassionate veterinary care, quality pharmaceutical services, and timely treatments to improve animal welfare.

Our Vision

To be the leader in innovative veterinary care, setting new standards for livestock healthcare in Somaliland.

Our Services

Pharmacy Services

High-quality veterinary medicines, professional prescriptions, and drug administration guidance.

Clinical Services

Routine health check-ups, disease treatment, vaccinations, and control programs.

Diagnostics

Blood tests, disease screening, and other diagnostic services for accurate treatment.

Consultations

Expert advice on nutrition, feed, livestock husbandry, and herd management.

Surgery & Procedures

Minor and routine surgical procedures, wound care, and emergency interventions.

Mobile & Field Services

On-farm services, emergency field interventions, and support for remote pastoralists.

  • Livestock Traders & Quarantines
  • Feedlots & Dairy Farms
  • Agricultural Cooperatives
